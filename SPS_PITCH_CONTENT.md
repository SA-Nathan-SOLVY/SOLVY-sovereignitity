# The SOLVY Data Flywheel: A New Revenue Stream for SPS

## Slide 1: The Opportunity
**Title:** Turning Transaction Data into Recurring Revenue
**Summary:** SPS can unlock a new revenue stream by leveraging the data already flowing through EBL's point-of-sale systems.
**Key Points:**
- **The Gap:** Currently, SPS sells products but loses visibility once they leave the warehouse.
- **The Solution:** The SOLVY Data Engine captures SKU-level data at the point of sale (EBL).
- **The Value:** This data creates "Reverse Inventory Feeds" for SPS and "Tax Automation" for members.
- **The Result:** A closed-loop ecosystem where data drives restocking and loyalty.

## Slide 2: How It Works (The Engine)
**Title:** The SOLVY Data Intelligence Engine
**Summary:** A seamless integration with Stripe that transforms raw payments into actionable intelligence.
**Key Points:**
- **Input:** EBL uses Stripe for payments. We add a simple "SKU Tag" to every sale.
- **Process:** The SOLVY Engine intercepts the transaction in real-time.
- **Categorization:** "Pedicure Chair" is automatically tagged as *Equipment* (Asset). "Acetone" is tagged as *Supplies* (Expense).
- **Output:** Instant, accurate data for both the Member and the Distributor.

## Slide 3: Value for the Member (The Pull)
**Title:** Automated Tax Savings for Beauty Professionals
**Summary:** Solving the #1 pain point for independent contractors: Bookkeeping.
**Key Points:**
- **Problem:** Nail techs hate saving receipts and guessing tax categories.
- **Solution:** SOLVY automates it. Every swipe builds their Schedule C tax form.
- **Benefit:** "I buy from EBL because SOLVY does my taxes for me."
- **Loyalty:** This creates a sticky ecosystem where members *prefer* SPS/EBL products.

## Slide 4: Value for SPS (The Push)
**Title:** Reverse Inventory & Demand Forecasting
**Summary:** Real-time visibility into what is actually selling at the salon level.
**Key Points:**
- **Visibility:** See exactly which products (SKUs) are moving in real-time.
- **Efficiency:** Automate restocking orders based on actual consumption, not just guesses.
- **Revenue:** Charge EBL a premium for "Just-in-Time" inventory management.
- **Data Monetization:** This aggregated demand data is valuable to manufacturers (OPI, Kupa, etc.).

## Slide 5: The Roadmap & Ask
**Title:** Launching the Pilot with EBL
**Summary:** We are ready to deploy this technology today using existing infrastructure.
**Key Points:**
- **Technology:** The Stripe Adapter and Data Engine are built and tested.
- **Pilot:** Deploy at EBL (Evergreen Beauty Lounge) to prove the model.
- **Ask:** SPS partnership to standardize the SKU catalog for the pilot.
- **Vision:** Scale this "Data Flywheel" to every salon in the SPS network.
