### I. CALL TO ORDER

The special meeting of the Members of **S.A. NATHAN LLC**, a Texas limited liability company (the "Company"), was called to order on **March 21, 2026**, by **Sean Mayo**, Managing Member.

### II. MEMBERS PRESENT

The following Members were present, constituting all of the Members of the Company and a quorum for the transaction of business:

| Member | Ownership (Prior) | Title (Prior) |

### III. PURPOSE OF MEETING

The purpose of this special meeting is to consider and approve the following matters:

1. Reorganization of the Company's management structure to ensure compliance with VA Total Disability Individual Unemployability (TDIU) requirements;
2. Assignment of membership interest from Sean Mayo to Evergreen Mayo to establish a 51%/49% ownership split;
3. Election of Evergreen Mayo as the sole Managing Member of the Company;
4. Designation of Sean Mayo as a Passive Member with limited rights as set forth in the Amended and Restated Operating Agreement;
5. Approval of the Amended and Restated Operating Agreement of the Company, effective March 21, 2026;
6. Approval of the Assignment of Interest by Sean Mayo to Evergreen Mayo, effective March 21, 2026;
7. Authorization to file a Certificate of Amendment with the Texas Secretary of State to reflect the current management structure;
8. Ratification of all prior actions taken by Members in the ordinary course of business.

### IV. DISCUSSION AND PRESENTATIONS

**Sean Mayo** presented to the Members the reorganization plan prepared to ensure his continued compliance with VA TDIU benefit requirements. The plan includes:

- Transitioning Sean Mayo from an active Managing Member role to a Passive Member role;
- Assigning 1% of Sean Mayo's membership interest to Evergreen Mayo, resulting in a 51%/49% ownership structure;
- Establishing Evergreen Mayo as the sole Managing Member with exclusive authority over all Company operations;
- Limiting Sean Mayo's role to: (a) identifying and vetting insurance carrier partners for future MOLI development, and (b) serving as steward of the SOVEREIGNITITY philosophy;
- Ensuring Sean Mayo receives only passive income distributions (K-1) and does not engage in day-to-day operations, management decisions, or substantial gainful employment.

**Evergreen Mayo** accepted nomination as the sole Managing Member and acknowledged her assumption of full operational responsibility for the SOLVY Ecosystem, including the SOLVY Card, DECIDEY education programs, and the Mandatory Audit Network (MAN).
