## 3. STEP-BY-STEP FILING INSTRUCTIONS

### OPTION A: ONLINE FILING (SOSDirect) ⭐ RECOMMENDED

**Fastest and most reliable method.**

**Step 1: Create or Log In to SOSDirect**
1. Visit: **https://direct.sos.state.tx.us**
2. If you don't have an account, click "New User? Sign Up" and create an account
3. If you have an account, log in with your username and password
4. Note: There is a $1.00 system search fee per search

**Step 2: Search for Your Entity**
1. Once logged in, select "Business Organizations" from the main menu
2. Click "Search"
3. Enter **"S.A. NATHAN LLC"** or file number **"805074128"**
4. Click "Search" and select your entity from the results

**Step 3: Initiate Amendment Filing**
1. On the entity details page, click **"File Amendment"** or **"Amend Certificate of Formation"**
2. Select **"Certificate of Amendment"** (not Restated Certificate unless you intend to restate the entire document)
3. Confirm the entity name: **S.A. NATHAN LLC**
4. Confirm the file number: **805074128**

**Step 4: Enter Amendment Information**
1. In the amendment text field, enter the following (copy exactly):

```
The Certificate of Formation is amended to reflect that the Company is manager-managed. As of March 21, 2026, Evergreen Mayo serves as the sole Managing Member with exclusive authority over the business and affairs of the Company. Sean Mayo holds a 49% membership interest and serves as a Passive Member with no authority over day-to-day operations or management decisions, consistent with the Company's Amended and Restated Operating Agreement effective March 21, 2026.
```

2. If any other provisions need amendment (e.g., purpose, duration), enter them in separate amendment fields
3. If the registered agent or office is changing, you may update it here OR file Form 401 separately

**Step 5: Review and Confirm**
1. Review all entered information carefully
2. Ensure the entity name and file number match exactly
3. Verify the amendment language is correct

**Step 6: Payment**
1. Enter payment information (credit card or eCheck)
2. Filing fee: **$150.00**
3. SOSDirect service fee: **$1.00**
4. Total: **$151.00**
5. Submit payment

**Step 7: Download Confirmation**
1. After submission, download and save the filing confirmation
2. The filed document will typically be available for download within minutes to a few hours
3. Save the filed Certificate of Amendment with your company records
