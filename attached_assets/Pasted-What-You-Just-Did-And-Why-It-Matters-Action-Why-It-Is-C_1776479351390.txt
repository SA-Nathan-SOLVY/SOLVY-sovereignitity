I have reviewed the current search results and can confirm there is no Cloudflare-specific DNS guide for AgentMail included in them. However, AgentMail provides the same three DNS record types for all providers: SPF (TXT), DKIM (CNAME), and DMARC (TXT) . Here are the step-by-step instructions for Cloudflare.

The Three Records You Need to Add
Record Type	Name/Host	Value	Purpose
TXT (SPF)	@ (your root domain)	v=spf1 include:agentmail.to ~all	Lists AgentMail as an authorized sender 
CNAME (DKIM)	agentmail._domainkey	dkim.agentmail.to	Cryptographic signing of emails 
TXT (DMARC)	_dmarc	v=DMARC1; p=reject; rua=mailto:reports@agentmail.to	Tells receivers to reject failed emails
