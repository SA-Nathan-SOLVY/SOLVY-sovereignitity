## 🟢 EXTERNAL MESSAGING (Public/Business)

### Mission Statement (External)
> **SOLVY (Solutions Valued You)** builds generational wealth infrastructure for families who survived slavery, displacement, and colonialism through cooperative economic ownership.

**Key Elements:**
- ✅ Cooperative economics
- ✅ Generational wealth
- ✅ ADOS, Indigenous, Global South focus
- ✅ 70/20/10 economic model
- ✅ The Sheila Mandate ("Leave them better than I received")
- ✅ Iron fist protection (concept, not specific source)

### What We Do (External)
SOLVY Ecosystem™ is a cooperative financial platform that:
1. Issues member-owned debit cards (SOLVY Card™)
2. Returns 70% of interchange revenue to members
3. Builds economic infrastructure for marginalized communities
4. Provides data sovereignty through local-first architecture

### Who We Serve (External)
- ADOS (American Descendants of Slavery)
- Indigenous peoples
- Global South colonial immigrants (Filipinos, Africans, Caribbean)
- Asian American communities
- All marginalized groups seeking economic sovereignty
