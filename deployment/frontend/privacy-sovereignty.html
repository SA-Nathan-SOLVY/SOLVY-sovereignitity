⚡ Sovereign by Design

Data sovereignty is not a promise.

It is enforced by our architecture. We built it that way on purpose. No trust required — only code.

🔐 What "Sovereignty" Means for You

  • Your transaction history lives on your device — not on our servers.
  • No central database stores your individual spending.
  • Only aggregated, anonymized totals ever leave your device.
  • We cannot see your data. No one can. Only you.
  • If our server is hacked, the attacker sees only totals — never your shopping history.

How It Works

Your Device (Browser) ├─ manDB.js → Stores full transactions locally (IndexedDB) ├─ aggregationService.js → Computes totals, strips all individual data └─ HTTPS POST → Sends: totalVolume, categorySums, memberHash Network └─ Encrypted TLS → Only aggregate fields travel over wire Central Server └─ SQLite → Stores: member_hash, total_volume, category_sums NEVER stores: merchant names, exact timestamps, tx IDs Admin Dashboard └─ Views only totals → "Members spent $125K this month" Cannot see: who bought what, where, or when
