### 5. NFC Payment Requirements Documentation ✅
**Agent:** Technical Specialist  
**Status:** Documented  
**File:** `NFC_PAYMENT_REQUIREMENTS.md`

**Requirements for Members:**
- **Tap-to-Pay (Issuing):** SOLVY Card™ supports NFC contactless payments
- **Accept Payments (Acquiring):** Requires separate merchant account

**Implementation Path:**
1. **Phase 1 (Launch):** Cards work for tap-to-pay purchases
2. **Phase 2 (6 months):** Enable member-to-member transfers
3. **Phase 3 (12 months):** Merchant acceptance for business members

**Technical Stack:**
- Unit.co provides card network connectivity
- Visa/Mastercard NFC enabled
- Mobile wallet support (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- No additional hardware needed for members

**Key Point:** Members can immediately use SOLVY Card™ for NFC tap-to-pay purchases. Accepting payments requires additional merchant setup (Square, Stripe, or direct acquiring).
