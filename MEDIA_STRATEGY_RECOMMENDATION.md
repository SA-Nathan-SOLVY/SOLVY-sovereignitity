**Date:** March 31, 2026  
**Context:** First Circle launch (June 19, 2026)

---

## 🎯 RECOMMENDATION: Start with Slideshow, Add Video Later

### Phase 1 (Now - June 2026): **Slideshow + Short Video Clips**
### Phase 2 (Post-launch): **Full Video Commercial**

---

## 📊 COMPARISON

| Factor | Slideshow | Video Commercial |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| **Cost** | $0 (DIY with Canva/Slides) | $5K-$50K (professional) |
| **Time to create** | 2-3 days | 2-4 weeks |
| **Production complexity** | Low | High |
| **Revision ease** | Easy (edit text) | Hard (re-shoot) |
| **Website performance** | Fast loading | Slower (bandwidth) |
| **Accessibility** | High (screen readers) | Lower (needs captions) |
| **Grant applications** | Preferred format | Often not accepted |
| **Investor decks** | Standard | Risky if too polished |
| **Social sharing** | Good | Better (engagement) |
| **Emotional impact** | Moderate | High |

## ✅ WHY SLIDESHOW FIRST

### 1. **Speed to Market**
- Launch is 80 days away (June 19)
- Slideshow can be ready in 3 days
- Video would rush production quality

### 2. **Budget Efficiency**
- Foundation budget: $20,000
- Professional video: $5,000-$15,000
- That’s 25-75% of your runway
- Slideshow: $0 (use Canva + Kimi content)

### 3. **Flexibility**
- Edit messaging as you learn
- A/B test different versions
- Update for different audiences (grants vs investors)

### 4. **Multi-Use Asset**
One slideshow becomes:
- Website hero section
- Grant application attachment
- Investor pitch deck
- Social media carousel
- Email newsletter content
- Conference presentation

### 5. **Kimi Integration**
You have **unlimited Slides mode** with Allegretto:
- Generate 5-10 slide variants
- A/B test messaging
- Create audience-specific versions
- No additional cost

## 🎬 VIDEO COMMERCIAL: WHEN & WHY

### When to Produce (Timeline)

| Milestone | Timing | Video Purpose |
|-----------|--------|---------------|
| **First 100 members** | July 2026 | Member testimonial compilation |
| **1,000 members** | December 2026 | Brand awareness campaign |
| **Series A funding** | 2027 | Investor storytelling |
| **National expansion** | 2028 | Mass market advertising |

### Why Video Then (Not Now)

1. **You need member stories**
   - Real testimonials > scripted actors
   - Authenticity matters for trust
   - First Circle members = content

2. **You need proof points**
   - "We have 1,000 members" > "We will have members"
   - Transaction volume data
   - Dividend distribution proof

3. **Budget will be different**
   - Post-launch: Revenue-funded
   - Now: Depletes critical runway

4. **Video quality expectations**
   - Amateur video hurts brand
   - Professional video requires:
     - Scriptwriter
     - Director
     - Camera crew
     - Editor
     - Voice talent
     - Music licensing
