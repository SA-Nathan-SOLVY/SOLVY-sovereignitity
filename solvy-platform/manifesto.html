On Self-Sovereign Identity

True sovereignty is not isolation. It is autonomy with protection. SOLVY implements this through:

Data Sovereignty: Your transaction history stays on your device, not in corporate databases. You control access. You control disclosure. You are not the product.

Economic Sovereignty: You own 70% of the revenue your transactions generate. Not as charity. Not as reward. As ownership.

Governance Sovereignty: One member = One vote. The protocol serves its users, not distant shareholders.

"The system that profits from your labor must carry responsibility for your survival. This is not ideology—it is structural stability."
