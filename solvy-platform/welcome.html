What Makes SOLVY Different

Three principles that define everything we build.

👑

Member Owned

SOLVY is a cooperative, not a corporation. Every member is an owner. You have a vote in how the cooperative operates, and you share in the profits through patronage dividends.

📈

Wealth Building

Every swipe of your SOLVY Card generates interchange revenue. 70% goes back to members as patronage dividends. Your everyday spending builds collective wealth for the cooperative.

🔒

Data Sovereignty

Your transaction history stays on your device — not our servers. We built a system where we cannot access your individual data, even if we wanted to. Privacy is architecture, not policy.

How It Works

Four steps from joining to earning.

1

Join

Complete member onboarding and identity verification (KYC). Become a cooperative owner.

2

Spend

Use your SOLVY Visa Card anywhere Visa is accepted. Groceries, gas, dining — every purchase counts.

3

Earn

Interchange revenue (about 1.5% per transaction) flows into the member pool automatically.

4

Profit

Receive quarterly patronage dividends based on your card usage. The more you use, the more you earn.

"Data sovereignty is not a promise.
It is enforced by our architecture."
  • Your full transaction history lives on your device (IndexedDB)
  • Only aggregated, anonymized totals ever leave your device
  • No central database stores your individual spending history
  • We cannot see your data. No one can. Only you.
