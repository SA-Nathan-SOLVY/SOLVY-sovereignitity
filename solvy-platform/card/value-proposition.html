SOLVY CARD™

What it is: A member-owned bill pay card that captures interchange revenue for the people who use it.

How it works:

  1. You pay your monthly bills ($5,000 average) using SOLVY Card
  2. SOLVY earns 1.5% interchange ($75 per month)
  3. That $75 goes back to the member-owned cooperative
  4. You get profit share, fee reductions, or cash back

What you get:

  • $900 per year in value (based on $5K/month spending)
  • Ownership stake in the cooperative
  • Vote on fees, rewards, and policies
  • No hidden markups

What the market gives you:

  • Points that expire
  • No ownership
  • No control
  • Value goes to the bank
