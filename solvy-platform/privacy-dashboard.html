🏗️ Technical Architecture

How SOLVY's local-first, privacy-centric data system works

📱 Local-First Storage

All transaction data is stored in IndexedDB on your device. No central database holds your personal transaction history.

📊 Client-Side Aggregation

Totals and averages are calculated on your device before sending. Only aggregates leave your device.

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Data contributions are encrypted with AES-256-GCM before transmission. Only cooperative managers can decrypt.

⏱️ Auto-Delete

Contributed data automatically deletes after 30 days. No backups, no retention beyond the agreed period.

🗳️ Threshold Voting

When cooperative volume reaches milestones ($500K, $750K, $1M), members vote on pooling data for bargaining.

🎭 Anonymous Identity

Member IDs are hashed with SHA-256. No link between your vote, your data contribution, and your identity.
