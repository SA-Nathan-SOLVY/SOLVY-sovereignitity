What is SOLVY?

Solutions Valued You

SOLVY is more than a debit card. It's a cooperative ecosystem where members own their financial future — not shareholders, not banks, but the people who use it every day.

SOLVY Ecosystem™

SOLVY Ecosystem™ is the cooperative financial platform that powers the SOLVY Card™. It's built on three core principles:

Member Ownership: When you join SOLVY Ecosystem™, you become a member-owner. You don't just use the service — you own it. Through our 70/20/10 economic model, 70% of all interchange revenue is returned to members as patronage dividends.

Data Sovereignty: Your transaction data belongs to you. We use local-first architecture to keep your data on your device, not in corporate servers. You control what you share and what stays private.

Generational Protection: Through MOLI (Membership Owned Life Insurance), we convert member premiums into cooperative-owned policies that build cash value — creating the iron fist of financial protection for families who survived slavery, displacement, and colonialism.

SOLVY Card™

SOLVY Card™ is the debit card product of SOLVY Ecosystem™. It's the iron fist in plastic — spending as protection, not consumption.

Every swipe generates interchange revenue. That revenue doesn't go to Wall Street. It goes back to you and your fellow members through our 70/20/10 distribution model:

  • 70% Patronage Dividends — Direct returns to members
  • 20% Community Pool — Collective benefits (group insurance, bulk purchasing)
  • 10% Operations — Platform maintenance and growth
