Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

SOLVY Ecosystem™ ("SOLVY," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how SA Nathan LLC collects, uses, shares, and safeguards your personal information when you use the SOLVY Card™, website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, address, date of birth, Social Security number, phone number, email address
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, transaction history, card usage data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide banking and card services through our partner Unit.co and Thread Bank

  • Communicate with you about your account and services
  • Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and illegal activity
  • Improve our Services and develop new features

4. Information Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Unit.co and Thread Bank: Our banking-as-a-service partner required to provide deposit accounts and card services

  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Restricted access to sensitive data (need-to-know basis)

6. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@ebl.beauty.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and as required by law. Federal banking regulations typically require retention for 5 years after account closure.
