## What Each Side Brings

### SOLVY Brings
- **Live card-issuing infrastructure** (Lithic sandbox active, Treasury Prime fallback)
- **Cooperative legal structure** (Texas LLC, 70/20/10 revenue model)
- **Fiat on-ramp** — members can spend USD anywhere Visa/Mastercard is accepted
- **Member governance tools** (MAN portal, DECIDEY education platform)
- **Pre-launch community** — 45 committed members, growing waitlist

### Guapcoin Brings
- **Established blockchain network** — own chain, PoS consensus
- **Community trust** — 9 years of building, media recognition
- **Crypto-native audience** — holders who understand wallets, staking, DeFi
- **Wealth-building narrative** — appreciation, circulation, generational wealth
- **Technical credibility** — one of the first Black-woman-led crypto projects

## Partnership Scenarios (Phased)

### Phase 1: Cross-Promotion (Immediate)
- Feature Guapcoin in SOLVY's "Future Integrations" roadmap
- Feature SOLVY in Guapcoin's merchant/partner directory
- Joint social content: "Two tools, one mission"

### Phase 2: Crypto-to-Fiat Bridge (6–12 months)
- Guapcoin holders can load GUAP → SOLVY Card via Baanx or AlchemyPay
- SOLVY members can buy GUAP with their card via AlchemyPay
- Revenue share on conversion fees: split 50/50 between ecosystems

### Phase 3: Cooperative Revenue Share (12–24 months)
- When SOLVY members opt to receive dividends in GUAP instead of USD
- Guapcoin treasury receives a portion of interchange for ecosystem development
- Joint grant pool for Black developer education
