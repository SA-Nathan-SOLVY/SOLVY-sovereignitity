The Problem

The financial system was built to extract, not to serve.

Fees, opaque terms, and data exploitation are not bugs—they are features. Those in charge have created conditions where working families absorb risk while shareholders capture profit. Economic uncertainty is rising. The old system offers no lifeboat.

The Belief (SOVEREIGNITITY)

We believe there is another way.

SOVEREIGNITITY™ is our guiding principle. It is the practice of combining data sovereignty, personal autonomy, and cooperative ownership into a life that is self-directed and resilient. It means you own your information. You own your financial decisions. And you share in the value you help create.

This is not theory. This is architecture.

The Solution (SOLVY Ecosystem)

SOLVY Ecosystem™ is a cooperative neobank built on this belief.

At its center is the SOLVY Card™ — a debit card that earns revenue from every swipe. Those interchange fees do not disappear into shareholder dividends. They flow back to the cooperative.

The Member Pool & MOLI

The 70% Member Pool is reserved for future member benefits, starting with MOLI (Membership Owned Life Insurance) — a member-owned life insurance product inspired by BOLI (Bank Owned Life Insurance), a tool banks use to fund employee benefits. We put that same structure in the hands of members.

MOLI is a stated goal, not yet operational. We will partner with a licensed carrier to bring it to life. Until then, the Member Pool is held transparently, reported monthly, and deployed only with member knowledge.

Transparency (MAN)

MAN (Mandatory Audit Network) is our member-only transparency portal. Every financial statement, every allocation, every expenditure is posted for members to review. No fine print. No hidden fees. Just radical transparency.

Education (DECIDEY)

DECIDEY is our educational platform. It teaches data sovereignty, cooperative economics, and financial autonomy.

(The name stands for Decentralized Empowerment Control Identity Data Economy of Yours — but you do not need to memorize that. Just know it is your pathway to SOVEREIGNITITY.)
