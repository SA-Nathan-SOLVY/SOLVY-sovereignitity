🔄 Platform Status Clarification

The following distinction applies to all SOLVY presentations and partnership materials dated prior to May 2026.

Web 2 — Operational

solvy.cards

The cooperative debit card platform built on Unit.co banking infrastructure. Live in sandbox. Production launch targeting June 19, 2026.

✅ Active Development
Web 3 — Future Phase

solvy.chain

Self-sovereign identity and blockchain-anchored cooperative governance layer. Not yet operational. Planned for Phase 3 (2027–2028).

⏸️ Not Operational

📋 What This Means for Partners

All current partnership agreements, pilot programs, and member onboarding flow through solvy.cards (Web 2) infrastructure:

  • SOLVY Card™ issuance via Unit.co / Thread Bank
  • Interchange revenue sharing (70/20/10 model)
  • Member dashboard and dividend tracking
  • SPS Joint Venture integration (Pilot #2)
  • Evergreen Beauty Lounge integration (Pilot #1)

solvy.chain references in presentations describe the future roadmap, not current capability.

⚠️ Disclaimer for Presentation Viewers

Some printed and PDF versions of SOLVY presentations may contain references to "solvy.chain" or blockchain-based features without explicit status labeling. This addendum supersedes any ambiguous language in prior materials.

  • No blockchain tokens have been issued.
  • No Web 3 wallet integration is currently available.
  • Self-sovereign identity (SSI) remains in research phase.
  • All member benefits are delivered via traditional banking rails (Visa/Mastercard).
