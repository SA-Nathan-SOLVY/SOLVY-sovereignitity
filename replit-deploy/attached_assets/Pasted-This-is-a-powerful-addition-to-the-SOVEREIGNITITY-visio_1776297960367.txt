## The Core Concept: Member-Owned Data Pooling

| Step | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **1. Capture** | Members choose to share specific data from their devices (spending habits, preferences, etc.) |
| **2. Scrape/Collect** | The system can also collect public or semi-public data relevant to the cooperative |
| **3. Pool** | Data is anonymized and aggregated into member-owned data pools |
| **4. Monetize** | The pool is sold or licensed to third parties (AI trainers, researchers, marketers) |
| **5. Distribute** | Revenue flows back to members who contributed (e.g., via the 70% Member Pool) |
