Version 1: Manifesto
Title: A Parallel Economy, Not a Rejection

The existing economy—built on extraction, ISDS arbitration, and resource grabs—has created enormous wealth. We do not deny that. We do not ask anyone to be ashamed of it. Many of us have benefited from it, directly or indirectly.

But not everyone could participate.

That is not an accident. The same mechanisms that built wealth for some—from regime change to subprime mortgages to investor-state lawsuits—locked others out.

SOLVY does not seek to tear down. We seek to build alongside.

We are creating a parallel economy governed by SOVEREIGNITITY: data sovereignty, cooperative ownership, and patient revenue. Not to replace what exists, but to offer a choice.

You can participate in the old economy. You can also own the new one.

We do not reject. We build.

Version 2: Heritage Page
Title: Our Heritage – Extraction and Parallel Economy

Acknowledgment

The wealth of nations—including the one we call home—has often been built through extraction. From colonial resource grabs to modern ISDS arbitration (Investor-State Dispute Settlement), corporations have sued sovereign nations for billions to protect profits from oil, gas, minerals, and land.

This system created jobs, funded pensions, and built stock markets. It also displaced communities, undermined democracies, and created the diaspora that now seeks a new way forward.

We are not here to judge. We are here to build.

SOLVY is a parallel economy, not a protest. We do not ask you to leave the old system. We ask you to also participate in a new one.

In the parallel economy:

Revenue comes from interchange fees, not resource extraction

Ownership is cooperative, not corporate

Data is sovereign, not surveilled

Profits flow 70% to members, not to shareholders

Disputes are resolved through transparent member governance (MAN) , not private arbitration

The old economy worked for some. The parallel economy works for everyone who chooses it.
