## 2. REVENUE MODEL

### 2.1 Primary Revenue: Interchange Fees

**Mechanism**: SOLVY Card transactions generate interchange revenue paid by merchants to the card network (Visa/Mastercard), shared with SOLVY Cooperative.

**Interchange Rate Assumptions**:
- **Card Present (swipe/tap)**: 1.40% + $0.10
- **Card Not Present (online)**: 1.80% + $0.10
- **Weighted Average**: 1.50% (based on 70% CP / 30% CNP mix)

**Revenue Calculation**:
```
Monthly Revenue = (Transaction Volume × 1.50%) - Unit.co Revenue Share
```

Unit.co typically retains ~0.30% for banking services, leaving SOLVY with ~1.20% net.

## 11. RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

### 11.1 Revenue Concentration Risk

**Risk**: 95% of revenue from interchange fees  
**Mitigation**: 
- Diversification into premium services (Year 2)
- Partnership revenue expansion
- Interest income on deposits (when rates favorable)

### 11.2 Member Concentration Risk

**Risk**: Top 10% of members generate 50% of volume  
**Mitigation**:
- Community building to reduce churn
- Tiered benefits to retain high-volume members
- Continuous new member acquisition

### 11.3 Regulatory Risk

**Risk**: MSB enforcement or new regulations  
**Mitigation**:
- Proactive compliance program
- Legal counsel on retainer
- Conservative reserve requirements

### 11.4 Banking Partner Risk

**Risk**: Unit.co or partner bank discontinues service  
**Mitigation**:
- "Switch Kit" preparation from Day 1
- Portable data formats
- Member communication plan
- Alternative provider research (Highnote as backup)
