🔒 Privacy Guarantees

  • Local-first aggregation: Totals and averages are computed on your device before anything leaves.
  • No PII: We never collect names, addresses, merchant details, or exact transaction times.
  • Anonymous hashes: Your member ID is replaced with a salted SHA-256 hash.
  • Opt-in only: You choose which pools to join. You can withdraw at any time.
  • 70/20/10 revenue split: 70% of every sale goes to the Member Pool shared by contributors.
