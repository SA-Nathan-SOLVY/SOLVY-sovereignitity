🔍 How Exploitation Is Disguised as Self-Expression

The gig economy doesn't offer freedom—it offers precarity with better branding. They call you a "free agent" to mask that you have no health insurance. They call it a "side hustle" to normalize that your main job doesn't pay enough. They call it "being your own boss" to hide that you're actually working for an algorithm that can deactivate you without appeal.

This is the moral vacuum: the instrumentalization of human beings. The system that profits from your labor refuses responsibility for your survival.

✊ What Islamic Economic Principles Teach Us

In Islamic economics, the one who profits must carry the responsibility for those who contribute to that profit. You cannot transfer all risk to the weaker party while monopolizing all upside. This isn't just unethical—it's structurally unstable.

SOLVY implements this principle: 70% of interchange revenue returns to members. The profit doesn't flow to distant shareholders. It flows to you. The one who spends owns the revenue their spending generates.
