## 💳 SOLVY CARD™ PRODUCT SPECIFICATIONS

### Product Type
Cooperative-owned debit card issued through Unit.co banking infrastructure

### Technology Stack
```
Frontend:     Progressive Web App (PWA)
Backend:      Node.js + Express
Database:     PostgreSQL (planned)
Banking API:  Unit.co
Card Network: Visa/Mastercard (via Thread Bank)
Identity:     Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) - Phase 2
Hosting:      Hetzner VPS + Replit (development)
Domain:       ebl.beauty / solvy.coop
```

### Revenue Model: Interchange Fee Sharing

**Transaction Flow:**
```
Member Purchase → Merchant → Card Network → Thread Bank
                                          ↓
                                    Interchange Fee (~1.5%)
                                          ↓
                                    SOLVY Share (~1.2%)
                                          ↓
                              ┌───────────┼───────────┐
                              ↓           ↓           ↓
                           70%         20%         10%
                        Members    Community   Operations
                        Dividends     Pool      Reserve
```

**Member Economics (Example):**
- Member monthly spending: $1,200
- Interchange generated: $18.00
- SOLVY share: $14.40
- Member dividend (70%): $10.08/month = $120.96/year
- **ROI on $100 equity:** 121%

**Current (Phase 1):**
- Local-first transaction storage (IndexedDB)
- Member-controlled privacy settings
- Opt-in data pooling

**Future (Phase 2 - Self-Sovereign Identity):**
- Decentralized identifiers (DIDs)
