## What is the GENIUS Act?

**Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for U.S. Stablecoins Act**

Introduced by Sen. Bill Hagerty (R-TN) — **passed Senate Banking Committee March 2026**

### Current Status (April 2026)
- **Senate:** Passed Banking Committee (18-6 vote)
- **Floor Vote:** Expected Spring/Summer 2026
- **House:** Companion bill pending
- **White House:** Trump administration supportive

### What GENIUS Would Do
1. **Regulate stablecoin issuers** (Tether, USDC, etc.)
2. **Require 1:1 reserves** (cash or Treasury bills)
3. **Mandate monthly audits** (public disclosure)
4. **Create federal/state dual framework** (federal for large issuers, state for small)
5. **Require AML/KYC compliance** (anti-money laundering, know-your-customer)
6. **Prohibit algorithmic stablecoins** (must be asset-backed)

### Key Requirements Summary
| Requirement | What It Means |
|-------------|---------------|
| 1:1 Reserves | Every $1 stablecoin = $1 in vault |
| Monthly Audits | Public proof of reserves |
| Redemption Rights | Exchange 1:1 for USD anytime |
| Issuer Licensing | Federal or state approval required |
| Consumer Protection | Disclosures, fraud protection |
