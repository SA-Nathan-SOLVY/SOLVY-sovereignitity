## What is H.R. 40?

**Commission to Study and Develop Reparation Proposals for African Americans Act**

First introduced by Rep. John Conyers (D-MI) in **1989** — every year for 30+ years.

### Current Status (April 2026)
- **House:** Reintroduced annually, currently in Judiciary Committee
- **Senate:** Companion bill (S.40) introduced by Sen. Cory Booker
- **Hearings:** Held 2019, 2021, 2024 — no floor vote
- **Scope:** Study only — not payment authorization

### What H.R. 40 Would Do
1. Establish a federal commission to study slavery's legacy
2. Examine ongoing discrimination (housing, education, criminal justice, wealth gap)
3. Develop reparations proposals
4. Recommend implementation

### The Wealth Gap H.R. 40 Documents
| Metric | White Households | Black Households | Gap |
|--------|------------------|------------------|-----|
| Median Wealth | $188,200 | $24,100 | **7.8x** |
| Homeownership | 72.7% | 44% | **28.7 pts** |
| Retirement Savings | $80,000 median | $35,000 median | **2.3x** |
| Intergenerational Wealth | Continuous | Disrupted (slavery → Jim Crow → redlining) | **Systemic** |
