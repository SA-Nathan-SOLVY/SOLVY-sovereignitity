## ğŸ“± Using Your Card

### **Contactless Payments (NFC)**
1. Hold card near payment terminal
2. Wait for confirmation beep/light
3. Transaction complete!
4. No PIN needed for <$50 (varies by merchant)

### **Chip & PIN**
1. Insert card into terminal
2. Enter 4-digit PIN
3. Remove card when prompted
4. Keep receipt

### **Online Purchases**
1. Enter card number
2. Enter expiration date
3. Enter CVV (3 digits on back)
4. Enter billing ZIP code
5. Complete purchase

### **Mobile Wallet**
- **Apple Pay**: Add to Wallet app
- **Google Pay**: Add to Google Pay app
- **Samsung Pay**: Add to Samsung Pay app
- Use phone/watch for contactless payments
